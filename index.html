What's Buggy?

Buggy is a chaotically fun Carnival event where teams come together to create carbon-fiber gravity trikes (imagine a torpedo but with a person inside) and push them up hills in a frenzied race. But the trike -- most commonly classified as a buggy -- is not only a baton to be passed around -- the driver inside must maneuver around a curvy road a sharp turn in order to pull their team ahead in the race!


It's thrilling! It's fascinating! It's riveting! It's buggy!
